• Hubs: Hubs are hardware devices that can have a number of devices or computers connected to them. They are normally used to connect together a number of devices to form a LAN. Such as a star netwrok and its main task is to take any data packet received at one of its ports and broadcasts it to every computer in the network.
  • Switches: Switches are similar to hubs but are much more efficient in the way that they distribute data packets. Switches connect a number of devices and computers to a LAN. Unlike a hub a switch checks the packet received and works out the destination.
  • Bridges: bridges are devices that connect one LAN to anotehr LAN. They are often used to connect different parts of a LAN to be used as a single LAN
  • Router: Routers enable data packets to be routed between different networks, for example for a LAn to join a WAN(wide area network)
